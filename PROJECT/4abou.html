About Me

Kent Liv, the creator of URDEA is a first year IT student in UCLM who struggles to understand programming and math. He lives in Lapu-Lapu City, and is tired of spending money to commute back and forth. However, Kent is a dedicated person when it comes to art and music. He usually spends his time practicing the guitar, and listening to music genres like: classical music, deathcore, metalcore, j-pop, k-indie, and rap. Kent is also unsure about his future as he has too many interests including programming. After the semester, he might think again about his decision in college.

Kent Liv has also participated in the Battle of the Bands 2023 in his university. It was one of his special moment in his life as he had gained a lot of experience just from practicing with experienced bandmates. After the semester, he wishes to play the music he made and have some gigs somewhere for fun the fun times and for experience also and at the same time, if he's lucky, it would be good to earn money with it. This website is also for sharing his talent. Overall, he just loves what his interests: making music, making art, reading, and sometimes singing.
